KE HOACH CAP NHAT VA TRIEN KHAI TINH NANG DUNG THU (TRIAL MODE)
PHAN MEM KE TOAN PRO v11.0.0
========================================================================
Ngay lap: 18/05/2026
Tac gia: NgocPhan / Thay Hung
Muc tieu: Tu dong hoa quy trình cap quyen dung thu 30 ngay cho khach hang 
          khi tai ve ma khong can can thiep thu cong, bao dam an toan, 
          bao mat va chong gian lan thoi gian.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
GIAI DOAN 1: KHAO SAT THUC TE & CHUAN BI (Thuc hien trong 1 ngay)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Kiem tra cau truc bang `licenses`:
   - Mo co so du lieu trong thu muc `ke_toan_data/` hoac kiem tra file `SQL.txt`.
   - Xac dinh chính xac ten cac truong du lieu dang co (vi du: license_type, 
     start_date, expire_date, status hoac ngon ngu tuong duong).
     * Ghi chu: Hien tai kien truc ghi nhan bang nay dang co 0 dong.

2. Khao sat module bao mat `core/security/`:
   - Xem lai logic doc va giai ma file key ban quyen hien tai de tìm vi trí 
     nhung hop ly (dat nhanh `if... else...` khi khong tim thay file key chính thuc).

------------------------------------------------------------------------
GIAI DOAN 2: PHAT TRIEN MA NGUON MOI (Thuc hien trong 2 ngay)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Bo sung logic khoi tao Dung thu tai `core/database/` hoac `core/security/`:
   - Thiet lap ham kiem tra neu bang `licenses` trong (0 dong) -> Tu dong 
     chen 1 dong moi:
     + license_type = 'TRIAL'
     + start_date = Thoi gian hien tai
     + expire_date = Thoi gian hien tai + 30 ngay
     + status = 'ACTIVE'

2. Xay dung co che chong gian lan thoi gian (Anti-cheat):
   - Tan dung thu muc `config/` de ghi log thoi gian chay gan nhat vao mot file 
     cau hinh ma hoa hoac file JSON an (vi du: `config/app_state.json`).
   - Logic: Neu `Thoi gian he thong hien tai` < `Thoi gian chay gan nhat trong log`
     -> Phat hien khach hang lui dong ho Windows -> Lap tuc chuyen status thanh 
     'LOCKED' va khoa phan mem.

3. Dieu huong luong khoi chay (Main Execution Flow):
   - Goi ham kiem tra ban quyen ngay tai dau ham `main()` truoc khi ve giao dien.
   - Neu con han dung thu: Cho vao phan mem, hien thi so ngay con lai tren thanh 
     tieu de (Window Title) de nhac nho khach hang (vi du: "Con 25 ngay dung thu").
   - Neu het han hoac bi khoa: Goi cua so `ActivationDialog` trong `views/dialogs/` 
     de chan toan bo bieu mau, yeu cau nhap Key kích hoat chính thuc.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
GIAI DOAN 3: KIEM THU TOAN DIEN - TESTING (Thuc hien trong 1-2 ngay)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Su dung cac thu muc test da co (`tests/unit/` va `tests/integration/`) de test:
- Test case 1 (Khach moi tai): Xoa sach bang `licenses` -> Chay phan mem xem 
  co tu dong cap 30 ngay dung thu khong.
- Test case 2 (Tru ngay dung thu): Chinh tang thoi gian may tinh len 3 ngay -> 
  Kiem tra giao dien xem co tru bot 3 ngay dung thu hay khong.
- Test case 3 (Hack thoi gian): Chinh lui thoi gian may tinh ve qua khu -> 
  Kiem tra xem he thong co phat hien va bao khoa phan mem khong.
- Test case 4 (Het han): Chinh thoi gian vuot qua 30 ngay -> Kiem tra xem 
  co chan giao dien va hien thong bao nhap key khong.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
GIAI DOAN 4: DONG GOI VA TRIEN KHAI (Thuc hien trong 1 ngay)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Don dep du lieu thu nghiem:
   - Xoa sach cac dong thu nghiem trong bang `licenses` de dua ve trang thai 
     0 dong goc truoc khi dong goi. Xoa file log thoi gian tam thoi.
2. Cap nhat tai lieu cam nang khach hang:
   - Bo sung dieu khoan va huong dan dung thu vao file `TERMS_OF_USE.md` hoac 
     file `Dong goi phan mem cho khach.txt`.
3. Bien dich ra file thuc thi (.EXE):
   - Su dung cong cu `pyinstaller` (trong requirements.txt) de dong goi toan bo 
     du an dua tren cau hinh duong dan tu file `pyinstaller_paths.txt`.
