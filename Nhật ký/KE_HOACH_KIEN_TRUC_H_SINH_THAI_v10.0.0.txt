PHAN II: THIET KE "O KHOA THONG MINH" (CORE SECURITY & LICENSE)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Thoi gian du kien: Ngay 3 - Ngay 4
* Cac buoc trien khai:
  1. Cap nhat cau truc bang `licenses` trong Database:
     - Them truong `activated_modules` (Kieu TEXT, luu chuoi JSON hoac danh sach nganh nghe duoc cap phep).
  2. Nang cap he thong tao va giai ma Key tai `core/security/` va `core/encryption/`:
     - Key moi se ma hoa thong tin: Ma may + Goi cuoc (Basic/Pro/Enterprise) + Danh sach Module Add-on.
  3. Viet ham `LicenseManager.verify_and_get_permissions()` de chay khi phan mem khoi dong.


PHAN III: XAY DUNG MA TRAN DIEU KHIEN (DYNAMIC UI INTERFACE)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Thoi gian du kien: Ngay 5 - Ngay 6
* Cac buoc trien khai:
  1. Tai lop giao dien chinh (Main Window): Bo co che add_tab cung nhac. Chuyen sang dung `DynamicTabManager`.
  2. Xay dung ham `update_tabs_by_license(license_info)`:
     - Xoa cac tab dac thu dang hien hanh.
     - Duyet qua danh sach module duoc phep de add vao QTabWidget (hoac bo khung UI dang dung).
  3. Thiet ke Menu Admin:
     - Vao menu `Cong cu` -> Them submenu `Tab tuy chon theo nganh nghe`.
     - Neu User_Role == 'admin': Hien thi danh sach Checkbox cac nganh nghe.
     - Su kien `on_checkbox_changed`: Tich vao phat nao, goi ham add/remove Tab ra giao dien ngay lap tuc ma khong can reset app.
